What are the age limits for enrolling at FSS?

Anyone above the age 6 can enroll and learn horseriding

What are the timings for the classes

Classes are one hour each. To book a time slot, please contact the owner.

What is the dresscode for the horse riding classes?

Make sure to wear thick pants, preferably horse riding breeches, along with boots. Other external gear can be provided at the campus, althought it is preferred that you bring your own.

How many horses are at FSS

There are over 10 horses at FSS, which are cared for by our team.
